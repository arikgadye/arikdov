Hey there, I dream of a slower, more compassionate world, one where we value the time of the people we love, the things we love, and life itself. Over the years, music has become a ruthlessly competitive profession to belong to - I am not in search of competition, music has and will always remain a spiritual act for me, connecting me with the world and the humans and nature that inhabit it. If you'd like to connect with me and get updates on my process and the work I am doing, feel free to sign up for my email list.
